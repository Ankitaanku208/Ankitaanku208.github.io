About Us

Cyber Awareness Hub was created by students and educators passionate about cybersecurity. Our goal is to make online safety accessible and engaging for all students. We empower students to become the first line of defense against cyber threats while building skills relevant for the future.

We collaborate with educators to provide classroom-ready material and practical exercises, making cybersecurity education easy to integrate into academic programs.
